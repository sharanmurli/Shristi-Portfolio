About Me

DISCOVERING MYSELF


As I pursued my studies in Mass Media, I discovered my interest in marketing. Its ability to link people with life-enhancing products and services captured my imagination. I was fascinated by its power and influence. As I continue to explore the vast and varied realms of marketing, I remain awestruck by its ability to transform lives. There is so much to learn, and my journey has just begun.


PASSIONATE ABOUT


My passion for photography started with a simple phone camera in 2016 and has since grown into a full-fledged love affair. Through photography, I have discovered a way of seeing the world that is entirely my own. Photography has become my gateway to a thousand different lives, each one more fascinating and fulfilling than the last. I'm always reminded of the vast possibilities that this art form has, whether I'm capturing the beauty of nature, the simplicity of street life, or the depth of human emotion.


MAKING AN IMPACT


I started VAMSH - Vision and Mission for Serving Humanity Initiative with an aim to help those who were struggling and in need of support. It has been the most rewarding and fulfilling decision of my life. Through VAMSH, I have been able to reach out to individuals and organizations in need by providing them with dry rations & funds. Witnessing the positive impact that VAMSH has had on so many lives is truly heartwarming and has reinforced my belief that helping others is not just a duty, but a privilege.
