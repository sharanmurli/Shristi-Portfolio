Brand Identity

2022

To choose a product or service and create a Visual Brand Identity for the Business. It should include brand values, colours, mood board, typography and logo.

My Visual Brand Identity project is about "La Terre Paradise" - a pottery store which combines the soul of a craftsman and the vision of an artist to offer a variety of styles from classic to trendy, modern or traditional for every taste and budget. The store and the handcrafted pieces are inspired by nature and have earthy tones. You will get to see the brand values, colours, mood board and logo created for "La Terre Paradise"
