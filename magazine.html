Photography Magazine

2022

To create a Fashion Magazine/Zine. It should include 5 sections i.e. Trend Reports, Lockdown Fashion, Style a Celebrity, Advertorial and Cover Stories revolving around one main theme.

Our group's central theme for Khoj Magazine/Zine is Ethnic/Indian Fashion. Khoj Magazine/Zine takes you on a journey about Ethnic/Indian fashion, with Vijayalakshmi Murli's memories,Trijiya and Divya Kumari's ethnic fashion fusion in Trend Reports, in Lockdown Fashion, Anuja Jain & yours truly showcasing how to combine comfort and fashion. It also covers an interview with Pooja Bhamrah, who explores the intriguing relationship between fashion and humans.
